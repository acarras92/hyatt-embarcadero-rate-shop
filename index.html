2. FORWARD DEMAND ENVIRONMENT

Where is the compression in the next 365 days? Market demand index + Market OTB across SF luxury comp set. Hover any cell for the date + value.
Reading the indicators: Market demand index = Lighthouse proprietary 0-100% score combining competitor booking pace, market airfare demand, citywide events, group blocks. Populated for the first 60 days forward (Lighthouse's published forecasting horizon — beyond that, signal-to-noise drops too far for them to publish). Values >90% = strong compression; 30-60% = normal; <30% = soft demand. Market OTB = comp set's percent of inventory already on the books across the full 365-day forward year.
Horizon caveat: Lighthouse populates Market demand for ~60 forward days only. The 365-day strip uses Market OTB for the back half. KPI #2 (days at market demand > 90%) is windowed to the 60-day demand horizon.
